About

Evidence first. Practical by design.

I am a property insurance practitioner working at the intersection of operations, actuarial analysis and risk management.

How I work

Over ten years in property insurance, my scope has moved from reserves and planning into pricing, product, underwriting, channel operations and system delivery. That range has taught me to see a metric not as an endpoint, but as part of a decision chain: how it is defined, what behaviour it creates, and whether the process can actually support it.

I value evidence, precise definitions and straightforward execution. When the problem crosses teams, I try to establish a shared view of the objective, make constraints visible and translate policy into steps that operations and technology can deliver.

Data sensitivity is central to my work. I look for signals that change a decision, validate them against business context, and build monitoring and review into the solution. A useful model should help people act—not merely explain the past.

Long-term themes

  • Connecting growth, risk and cost in insurance operating decisions.
  • Turning actuarial and risk frameworks into usable front-line mechanisms.
  • Designing products and processes that remain clear under scale.
  • Improving cross-functional delivery among business, actuarial, underwriting and technology teams.
  • Documenting lessons so that a project becomes reusable organizational knowledge.

Professional foundation

I hold the FCAA, FRM and CPA credentials and was recognized as a Shenzhen High-level Professional. I earned a Master of Finance and a Bachelor of Insurance from Hunan University.
